Subversion Participants

We are putting together a coalition of developers interested in building a better version control system for the open source community. The following people are already working toward that goal. We welcome your participation.

Note that this is not a complete list -- it's just the names that whoever happened to update this page remembered at the time. If your name should be here but isn't, please email dev@subversion.tigris.org

Since the above list is not always up-to-date, you should subscribe to the the developer's mailing list if you want keep up with things. To do so, send email to dev-subscribe@subversion.tigris.org.

The first thing you should do when you join the list is to post an introduction message that tells the group:

  • who you are,
  • your interest in version control,
  • the role you would like to play in the project (e.g., do you want to just discuss ideas, write code, do design, or be an early users?), and
  • the rough level of effort you expect to put (e.g., mainly lurking, a few postings a week, a few evenings, several hours a week, this is part of your full-time job).

See the mailing list archive to see how others have introduced themselves.
